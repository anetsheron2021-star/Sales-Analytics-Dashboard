 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI Sales Dashboard** designed to analyze retail sales performance using a practice retail dataset in the context of a fictional.

The dashboard provides business stakeholders with a centralized view of key sales metrics, product performance, customer purchasing behavior, payment preferences, and regional performance to support data-driven decision-making.

Business Problem

Retail businesses often struggle to monitor multiple business metrics from different sources. Without a centralized dashboard, it becomes difficult to:

- Track monthly sales and profit performance.
- Identify profitable products and categories.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Analyze preferred payment methods.
- Compare sales performance across different states.
- Identify high-value customers for targeted marketing.

This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing an interactive and easy-to-understand business intelligence solution.

 Project Objective
The objective of this project is to build an interactive Power BI dashboard that enables decision-makers to:
- Monitor overall business performance.
- Track monthly profit trends.
- Analyze category-wise sales distribution.
- Identify the most profitable product sub-categories.
- Understand customer payment preferences.
- Compare regional sales performance.
- Identify top customers contributing to revenue.
- Perform state-wise analysis using interactive slicers.\
  
 Dashboard Features
-  Monthly Profit Analysis
-  Total Sales KPI
-  Total Quantity Sold
-  State-wise Sales Analysis
-  Quantity by Category
-  Payment Mode Distribution
-  Profit by Sub-Category
-  Top Customer Analysis
-  Interactive State Filter (Slicer)

 Key Business Insights
 Sales Performance
- Generated **₹438K** in total sales.
- Sold over **6K products** across multiple cities.
- Monthly profit fluctuates throughout the year with both profitable and loss-making months.

 Category Analysis
- Clothing contributes the highest share of products sold.
- Furniture and Electronics contribute comparatively lower sales volume.
- 
 Profitability Analysis
- Bookcases generated the highest profit among the displayed sub-categories.
- High sales volume does not always translate into high profitability.

 Payment Analysis
- Cash on Delivery (COD) is the most preferred payment method.
- UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card and EMI contribute the remaining transactions.

 Regional Analysis
- The dashboard enables state-wise sales analysis using an interactive slicer.
- Users can quickly identify top-performing customers and sales for individual states.



 Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-demand categories such as Clothing.
- Promote high-margin products like Bookcases to improve profitability.
- Investigate months with negative profit and optimize pricing or operational costs.
- Encourage prepaid payment methods through cashback or discount campaigns.
- Reward high-value customers with loyalty programs and personalized offers.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing states while exploring growth opportunities in lower-performing regions.
